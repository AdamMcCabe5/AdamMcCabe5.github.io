What is Dragon Ball Z?

Dragon Ball Z is a japanese animated or anime tv series made by Toei Animation. Its a sequal to Dragon Ball adapts the latter chapters of the Dragon Ball manga (japanese comic) created by Akira Toriyama. Dragon Ball Z aired in Japan from April 1989 to January 1996, before getting dubbed in territories including the United States, Canada, Australia, Europe, Asia, India and Latin America. It was broadcast in at least 81 countries worldwide. Its probably the biggest part of the Dragon Ball media franchise

What is the story of Dragon Ball Z?

The story mostly follows Goku who with the help of his friends help defend earth and the universe from aliens like Frieza, andriods like Cell and mysthical creatures like Majin Buu. The story also parallels the life of Gohan (Goku's son) and Vegeta (An enemy at the start of the show) and shows their development
